About Us

Revolutionizing The Learning Experience

CTU-Buddy is an advanced educational system with the aim of transforming the learning experience at City Technical University (CTU). As a comprehensive and interactive website, CTU-Buddy acts as a centralized platform for students, offering a wide array of essential tools and resources to elevate their academic achievement.

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop and maintain a user-friendly platform that gives students the tools and resources they require to manage their schedules, engage in meaningful discussions and share educational resources with their peers and educators

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a dynamic, inclusive online learning environment that encourages collaborative learning, empowers students, and facilitates academic success

Our Value

Welcome to CTU-Buddy! By offering an interactive educational platform, our purpose is to improve the learning experience of all students at City Technical University (CTU)

Key Features Of The CTU-Buddy

  • Timetable management System
  • Resource sharing platform
  • Discussion forum
